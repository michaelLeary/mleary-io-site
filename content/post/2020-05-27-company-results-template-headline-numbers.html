Headine figures

Suzuki announced a 10% reduction in net sales for the year to JPY 3488 billion or US$32.4 billion. Operating income fell 34% to JPY 215 billion or US$2 billion. Earnings per share declined 28% to JPY286/share. Despite the reported decline registered and further weakness projected ahead the company balance sheet remains strong and management declared a special dividend.
