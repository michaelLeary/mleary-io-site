Remap factor levels

michael

2020/06/02

Further to data extract of human muscle group from Wikipedia the goal here was to remap the factor levels in the variable source. The intention is to shrink down the number of categories from 48 to 5 to simplify understanding of statistics to be performed on muscle groups in future.

To achieve that the source variable in the data frame was converted to factor type. It was then duplicated. A fresh factor variable that mapped the levels from the duplicated source variable was created and is labelled x.
